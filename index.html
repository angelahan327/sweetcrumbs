About SweetCrumbs

SweetCrumbs began when a group of college friends shared a passion for baking. In 2026 we opened our doors in Maplewood to share handcrafted cakes, pastries, and breads made from scratch with quality local ingredients. No preservatives, no shortcuts—just the kind of treats that make every day a little sweeter.
